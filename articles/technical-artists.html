ISUX高规格特聘“技术美术”人才
EVENT
我们以最高规格诚意特聘！

腾讯QQ Studios，ISUX设计团队正在寻找具有才华横溢和热情的技术美术师。这些职位的关键作用是开发强大的内容制作管道，并通过集成工程框架帮助实现视觉设计的还原度。这是为了确保通过支持工具的应用，简化工作流程，高效生成高质量的可用原型。

我们以最高规格诚意特聘！

Tencent QQ Studios, ISUX design team is currently looking for talented and keen Technical Artists. The key roles of these positions is to develop a strong backbone of content production pipeline, helping to realize the fidelity of visual design with the integration of engineering framework. This is to ensure a streamlined workflow with supporting tools that produces visually appealing and highly efficient working prototypes.

We are eagerly awaiting you to join us.


工作经验

•至少3年的技术美术或相关设计/工程经验

•扎实的OpenGL / WebGL经验

•出色的工作经验，以动态图形或最小可行产品的形式展示可视化盒高质量设计演示的能力

•具有虚幻引擎/Unity的经验优先考虑


美术base

至少具备以下经验之一

•实验，计算和生成艺术设计，包括GAN

•3D建模，包括PBR纹理输出

•2D / 3D运动图形设计，包括3D模型骨骼驱动和动画

•视觉效果设计，包括合成、色调映射和程序交互设计


技术base

至少具备以下经验之一

•基于节点的着色语言或OpenGL着色语言

•物理模拟计算，包括碰撞、重力和布料模拟

•光照和后处理，包括程序调色和渲染

•定义和构建美术管道的工作流程


工作地点：

•深圳/上海/北京/首尔


主要职责：

•与设计师和工程师紧密合作，判断美术管道的工作流程和构建问题，分析并提供可行的解决方案以实施高质量的设计演示

•与其他领域的技术美术师合作，提供整体解决方案，确保遵循最佳实践

•为设计师提供设计素材输出的优化以适配技术框架的要求

•弥合设计师，工程师和产品经理的期望之间的差距，以不断超越交付成果的要求标准

•引领研发工作，以定义、完善和维护高效的工作流程


Work Experience:

•At least 3 years of experience as a technical artist or related design/engineering role

•Solid knowledge of OpenGL/WebGL is essential

•Outstanding portfolio of work experience which demonstrates the ability to visualize high quality design demos in the form of motion graphics or minimum viable products

•Knowledge of Unreal Engine/Unity is a plus


Art-based

At least one experience of the below

•Experimental, computational and generative art design, including GAN

•3D modelling including PBR texturing

•2D/3D motion graphics design, including 3D model rigging and animation

•Visual effects design, including compositing, tonemapping and procedural interactive design


Technical-based

At least one experience of the below

•Node-based shading language or OpenGL Shading Language

•Computational physics simulation, including collision, gravity and cloth simulation

•Lighting and postprocessing, including procedural color grading and rendering

•Defining and building workflow for art pipeline


Working location

•Shenzhen/Shanghai/Beijing/Seoul


Key Responsibilities:

•Work closely with designers and engineers to identify art pipeline’s workflow and build problems, analyse and provide viable solutions to implement high quality design demos

•Collaborate with other Technical Artists of different disciplines to provide holistic solutions, ensuring best practices are followed

•Advise designers on the optimization of design assets to fit within the technical framework

•Bridging the gap between the expectation of designers, engineers and product managers to continuously surpass the requirement standard of deliverables

•Lead research and development efforts to define, refine and maintain efficient workflow


你需要：

•具有敏锐的审美观

•创意，创新，思想开放

•上进心和对质量的痴迷


You need to:

•Have a keen sense aesthetics

•Be creative, innovative and open-minded

•Be self-motivated and obsessed with quality
